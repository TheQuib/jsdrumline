## Daily

### Warmups

{{< notice "note" >}} 5-10 second break in between {{< /notice >}}

 - 30 second jog in-place
 - 30 trunk twists, 15 each way
 - 15 jumping jacks
 - 30 fire hydrants, 15 each leg
 - 15 glue bridges
 - 15 second cobra pose (upward dog)
 - 15 second child pose, deep breaths

### High Intensity Interval Training

{{< notice "note" >}} 20 second break between each {{< /notice >}}

 - 40 seconds of shuffles
 - 40 second forward-backward jogging, push off the right
 - 40 second superman hold
 - 40 second core hollowing (opposite of superman)

### Cardio

{{< notice "note" >}} On a scale from 1-10 (1 being little efford, 10 maximum effort), work between 4 and 8. <br><br> Start with 4 rounds, with each session , we'll work up to 8 rounds {{< /notice >}}

 - Bikes: 30 second sprint, 30 second walk

 ## 2 days/week

 ### Back/Chest/Abs

 {{< notice "note" >}} All 2 sets, 12-15 reps <br><br>Start with 12 reps. With each week, work up to 15 reps {{< /notice >}}

  - Chest Press Machine
  - Lat pulldown machine
  - Abs crunch machine
  - Abs twist machine
  - Back extension

<br><br>

## 1 day/week

### Legs/Abs

 {{< notice "note" >}} All 2 sets, 12-15 reps <br><br>Start with 12 reps. With each week, work up to 15 reps {{< /notice >}}

  - Hip adductor machine
  - Hip abductor machine
  - Leg curl machine
  - Leg extension machine
  - Calf extension machine
  - Glutes
  - Leg press machine
    - First set is a warm up, use lighter weight than working sets.
